About Cipher

Cipher is a student-built website designed to make language learning accessible and fun. It uses Python, AI technologies, and a responsive UI to offer a personalized experience.

The website was developed over the course of a year as part of Lara Gurses's Maturarbeit project and was inspired by her own struggles to learn new languages.

The future of Cipher is open, with possibilities of Mobile App expansion, as well as overall improvements to the website. Thanks for visiting! :)
